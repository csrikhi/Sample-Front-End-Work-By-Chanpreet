Recent epidemiological studies have reported that migrant Asian Indians living in different parts of the world show a much higher prevalence of diabetes and other chronic degenerative disorders like heart disease than the host populations of those countries. And the prevalence of diabetes in the urban metros of India is approaching the figure reported in the affluent migrant Indians. Therefore there is an urgent need to understand health and adopt a healthy diet, physical activity to prevent chronic diseases. The initial tabs of this website outline the health risks and types of chronic disease prevalent among Asian Indians and why is it important to prevent. Also has detailed information on simple and healthy eating methods, simple techniques of physical activity, and lots of freebies to download and make your body healthy.
