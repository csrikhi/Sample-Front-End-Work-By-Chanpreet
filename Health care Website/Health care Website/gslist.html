Grocery Shopping List


→Vegitables:
   ºBroccoli
   ºGreen beans
   ºAsparagus
   ºBaby carrots (dip this with PB, so good!)
   ºTomatoes
   ºCucumbers
   ºRomaine lettuce
   ºBaby spinach
   ºOnions
   ºRed peppers
   ºMushrooms (all kinds! and this really should be under fungi)
   ºSeeweed
   ºSweet potatoe
   ºFrozen edamame, shelled (you can get this at Trader Joes or Asian food markets)


→Fruits
    Any that are in season!
   ºBananas
   ºBlueberries
   ºStraberries
   ºMangoes
   ºCantaloupe
   ºHoneydews
   ºNectarines
   ºAvocados
   ºChikoo
   ºGuava
   ºIndian Gooseberry
   ºCoconut
   ºLichee

→Grains/ Bread/Noodles:
   ºOatmeal – steel cut, rolled, or old fashioned
   ºQuinoa
   ºJowar, Bajra,Sorghum, Milllets
   ºBrown rice
   ºWhole wheat/whole rains breads

→Protein sources:
   ºEgg whites, also egg yolk (yellow part also)
   ºChicken breast
   ºTofu
   ºTVP textured vegetable protein
   ºSeafood: white fish, salmon, scallops, canned tuna in water
   ºLentils
   ºBlack beans


→Nuts/Seeds:
    ºGround flax seed
    ºRaw unsalted sunflower seeds
    ºRoasted and unsalted almonds
    ºPitaschios
    ºFreshly ground unsalted peanut butter from Whole Foods


→Dairy And Dairy Substitutes:
    ºNonfat or low fat 1-2% cottage cheese
    ºNon fat plain Greek yogurt
    ºAlmond Breeze almond milk, unsweetened vanilla


→Supplements:
    ºVanilla whey protein – Each scoop should have at least 20g of protein!

    ºOther options are soy protein, rice protein, pea protein, egg protein, and casein
    ºNon fat plain Greek yogurt

→Spices:
    ºCardamom (Elaichi)
    ºChilli
    ºCinnamon (Dalchini)
    ºCloves (Lavang)
    ºCumin (Jeera)
    ºCurry leaves
    ºCurry powder
    ºGaram Masala
    ºGarlic
    ºGinger
    ºSaffron
    ºTumeric
    ºPoppy Seeds (Kus Kus)
    ºMint Leaves (pudina)
    ºAsafoteda (Hing)
    ºLemon –try to squeeze it on top of anything.
